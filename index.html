About Realstate Solutions

The real estate industry encompasses the many facets of property, including development, appraisal, marketing, selling, leasing, and management of commercial, industrial, residential, and agricultural properties. This industry can fluctuate depending on the national and local economies, although it remains somewhat consistent due to the fact that people always need homes and businesses always need office space.

Realstate is a platform where you can find the house that you are looking for anywhere in Kenya. This site is aimed at easing the tedious, grueling and costly activity of having to go around looking for the appartments of your choise. This site includes pictures of the houses as well as a link to a conversation with the owner/management of the houses for easy communication and negotiation.
